Too Much to Say

Posted April 10 2008 7:40PM by Michael

There genuinely is just too much information for me to regurgitate into this blog, but I will try my best (now that I have turned in my homework) to put down some of my thoughts:

  • Troy Tulowitzki walks to the plate to the sounds of 'I'm a flirt' by R. Kelly. I don't know how to describe this, except for the fact that it makes him even better.
  • I am still doing my best to figure out how to get my hands on an authentic Arrelious Benn jersey, but it doesn't even look like the athletic department can help me out (pending a response from one last person that I remain hopeful will respond positively).

Therefore, due to my love of Illinois football, they should be begging me to proudly wear an authentic jersey (PLEASE).
  • I have purchased the majority of parts to build a massive server, including two 1 terabyte hard drives running (likely) RAID 1. The website may be down a little this weekend as I try to transition to that.

You will also notice a slight change in the fonts on the site, now that I am back online I remembered that I needed to play around with that a little to make it less boring.
